Make New Cutting Boards
August 5,2016

To make your cutting boards look like new, vigorously rub some salt into them. You can also treat wooden cutting boards with a very light coating of mineral oil. Be careful not ot overdo it, because mineral oil may affect the potency of a number of vitamins in fruits and vegetables.

Grease Pans like Pros
August 5, 2016

Recipes for baked goods often call for greased and floured pans, which usually involves oiling down the pan, then sprinkling flour inside and shaking until it's equally distributed. Professionals use a different way. Instead, they make something called batch of "baker's magic," and now you can too. Mix 1/2 cup room temperature vegetable shortening, 1/2 cup vegetable oil, and 1/2 cup all-purpose flour. Blend the mixture well and use it to grease pans. You can refrigerate it in an airtight container for up to six months.
